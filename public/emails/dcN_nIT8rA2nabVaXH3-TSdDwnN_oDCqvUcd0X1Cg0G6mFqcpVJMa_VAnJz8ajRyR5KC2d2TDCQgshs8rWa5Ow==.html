Why does this matter?

Having both account and data recovery options ensures that you can always regain access to your services and data, even if something unexpected happens. Remember, not even Proton can recover your data if you lose access without these options set.

Here’s what you can do:

  1. Visit your Account Safety Review page.
  2. Review your account’s security settings.
  3. Follow the prompts to set up or update your recovery methods.

By going through the Account Safety Review, you’ll make sure that your account and data are fully protected, allowing you to continue using Proton with confidence.
